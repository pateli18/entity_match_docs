### 2. Start a Categorization Job

<Snippet file="app-start-job.mdx" />

<Frame caption="Start Categorize Job">
  <img src="/screenshots/app/categorize_job.png" alt="Start a categorize job" />
</Frame>

1. Select `New Job`
1. Select `Categorize`
1. Enter a name for your job - this can be anything you want
1. Select the `dataset` you added in the previous step from the dropdown
1. Enter the specific codes for your job in the `Categories` field. You can enter multiple codes at once by separating them with a `|` character.
1. If you want to give the app specific guidance, click `Edit` on the `Instructions` box. While not strictly necessary, this can help guide the app to code your data more accurately.

### 3. Exporting Your Codes

<Snippet file="app-job-progress.mdx" />

Once the categorize job is complete, you can click the `Export Data` button to get the corresponding code values in a CSV file.
